# Product Backlog

## User Stories

| # | User Story | Priority | Story Points | Acceptance Criteria |

| US-01 | As a resident, I want to submit a complaint online so that I don't have to go to the barangay hall in person. | High | 5 | Complaint form is accessible, fields are validated, submission is confirmed. |
| US-02 | As a resident, I want to track the status of my complaint so that I know if it's being addressed. | High | 3 | Resident can view complaint status (Pending, In Progress, Resolved). |
| US-03 | As a barangay officer, I want to view all submitted complaints so that I can manage them efficiently. | High | 5 | Officer dashboard shows all complaints with filters by status and date. |
| US-04 | As a barangay officer, I want to update the status of a complaint so that residents are informed of progress. | High | 3 | Officer can change status; resident sees the update. |
| US-05 | As a resident, I want to register an account so that my complaints are linked to my identity. | High | 3 | Registration form works, account is created, login is possible. |
| US-06 | As a resident, I want to log in securely so that only I can see my complaints. | High | 2 | Login requires email and password, wrong credentials are rejected. |
| US-07 | As a barangay officer, I want to assign complaints to specific staff so that responsibilities are clear. | Medium | 3 | Officer can assign complaint to a staff member from a list. |
| US-08 | As a resident, I want to receive a notification when my complaint status changes so that I am always updated. | Medium | 5 | Email or in-app notification is sent when status changes. |
| US-09 | As an admin, I want to generate a report of complaints so that I can present data to barangay officials. | Medium | 5 | Report shows total complaints, statuses, and resolution rate. |
| US-10 | As a resident, I want to attach a photo to my complaint so that the issue is clearly shown. | Low | 3 | Resident can upload an image file; it is saved and viewable by officers. |
